About Koomapatti

Koomapatti is a village in Watrap taluk, Virudhunagar district, Tamil Nadu, situated just 5 km west of Vathirairuppu. The village, set against the majestic Western Ghats, has a population of around 12,700 and is governed as per the local government system of Tamil Nadu.

Koomapatti has recently gained viral fame on social media, with many comparing its picturesque beauty to Kashmir.
